In this form, you can enter the period limits of the calculated totals for the accumulation registers or accounting registers.

By default, the following dates are suggested to set the limit of calculated totals:

  • For accumulation registers is the end of the previous month.
  • For accounting registers is the end of the current month.

Dates can be filled in manually or by clicking .

After the change, the date is automatically converted to the end date of the month of the selected date.

If the command for setting the period of calculated totals is selected for one register, only one parameter matching to the register type can be changed.
