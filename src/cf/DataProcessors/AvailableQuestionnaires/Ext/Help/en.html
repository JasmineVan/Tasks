Use this window to view and edit questionnaires available to the respondent.

When the respondent starts the application, they see the Available questionnaires list. The list contains questionnaire descriptions.

If a survey is time-limited, the deadline by which it must be filled in will be shown in the list of available questionnaires after the questionnaire name.

If a questionnaire was filled in before but was not completed (the author allowed filling it out at different times), the date of its latest edit is specified in the list of available questionnaires.

The respondent can see previously filled questionnaires by clicking Questionnaire archive.
