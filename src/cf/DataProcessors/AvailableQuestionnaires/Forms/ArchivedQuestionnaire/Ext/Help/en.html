Use this window to view completed questionnaires.

It is opened from the respondent desktop by clicking Questionnaire archive.

The list includes questionnaires with the Show in questionnaire archive check box selected by the author.

The list includes the following columns:

  • Description is the questionnaire description.
  • Filling date is the date and time of questionnaire completion.

View questionnaire

  • To view the questionnaire, select it in the list and click Select.
  • You can also double-click the item.
