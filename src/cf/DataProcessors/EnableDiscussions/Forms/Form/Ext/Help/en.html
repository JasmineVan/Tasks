This form is used to enable conversations.

Application users can exchange text messages, make video calls, or create conversations to discuss a variety of topics.

To enable conversations for the users, establish connection to the 1C:Dialog server.

To open the form, click Connect in the Administration – Online user support and services – Conversations section.

Enable discussions

  • Type in an email address. A registration code will be sent to this address.
    • The infobase will be linked to this email address, and the infobase details will be submitted to the 1C:Dialog server.
  • Click Register.
  • The Registration code was sent to (the email address specified above) message will be displayed.
    • CLick OK.
    • Check the email account for a message with the registration code.
  • Type in the registration code in the Enable conversations window. Click Register.
  • Conversations will be enabled. This will allow the infobase users to communicate.
  • To close the form, click Done.
