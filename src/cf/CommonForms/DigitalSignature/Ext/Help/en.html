Use this form to view the general properties of a digital signature.

Digital signature verifies that an electronic document was not altered in any way since the moment it was signed. Electronic document integrity is controlled by checking a correspondence between a digital signature and a document and the certificate validity. Integrity control of an electronic document can be performed at any moment of time.

The digital signature allows you to:

  • Sign the application objects (for example, the Description and Summary fields)
  • Sign the content of the stored files

Signature information

  • Signature author - the name of the person the certificate was issued to
  • Signed by - the name of the person that applied the signature (filled automatically)
  • Signature date - date and time the signature was applied
  • Comment - additional signature information

Exporting a signature

  • To save the digital signature on a computer, click Save to file and specify a local or network folder.

Viewing a certificate

  • To view the properties of a digital signature key certificate, click Open certificate.
