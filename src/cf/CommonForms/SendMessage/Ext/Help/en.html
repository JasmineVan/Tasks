Use this window to send emails.

Buttons New email and Send are available in a variety of applied objects, such as application user window, and more.

Writing an email

  • In the To field, you can specify multiple email addresses separated by comma. Email address examples:
    • johnson@server - brief format, includes email address only.
    • Johnson johnson@server - full format includes both name and email address.
    • johnson (this requires mail server capability to look up email addresses from brief names).
  • Fill in the Subj field.
  • Enter the message text. To format the text, use standard formatting commands. The most popular commands are shown as icons above the message text. Other commands are available in the More menu.
  • To attach additional files to your email message, click Attach file.
    • To delete an attachment, select it by left-clicking and press Del.
  • An email account preconfigured for automated email exchange is suggested automatically in the From field.
  • In the Reply to field, you can specify an email address that will be automatically inserted into the To field when a user replies to your email message. This field should be filled in if you do not want to receive replies to your email (this can be useful when you send emails from a send-only account, or cannot view the incoming emails for any reason).

Sending

  • When you are ready to send the email, click Send.
