The report is designed to view statistics on the selected scheduled job.

To open it, double-click the scheduled job name from the Event log analysis – Duration of scheduled jobs report.

The report displays the following information:

  • Total duration
  • Number of launches
  • Number of canceled jobs
  • Number or errors

You can also see information on scheduled job sessions, their duration, and posting time.

Editing job schedule

  • Click Change schedule, and fill in required parameters.

View event log

  • Click Go to event log to view data on scheduled job sessions in the event log.
