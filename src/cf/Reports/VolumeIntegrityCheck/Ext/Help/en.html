This form opens from the File storage volumes card when you click Check integrity.

You will see the total number of files stored in the volume, percent of integral data, missing data, and unnecessary files in numbers and as a diagram for illustrative purposes.

Click Settings to use features of a finer records filter of the report.

Filtering by values

  • You can filter files by required parameters. Double-click the required value in the diagram.
  • You can refine your filtering criteria by selecting different fields from the list.
  • After you finish customization, click Select.
