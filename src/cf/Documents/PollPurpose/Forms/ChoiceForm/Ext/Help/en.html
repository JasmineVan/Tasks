Use this list to select the Survey assignment document.

To open the list, click the selection button in Survey analysis report or Analytical questionnaire report in the Survey section.

The list includes the following columns:

  • Number. The number is assigned automatically.
  • Date. Date and time the Survey assignment document was created.
  • Description. Full description of the document.

Selecting Survey assignment document

  • Select a document and click Select.
  • You can also double-click the item.

Filter by period

  • Click More actions - Set date interval and select a period to view documents.

Assigning a new survey

  • Click Create and fill in the mandatory fields.

Creating a new survey

  • Execute the Post command to create a new survey with the specified parameters. Respondents get a chance to fill in a questionnaire for this survey.

Canceling a survey

  • To cancel a survey, click More actions – Cancel. After that, the questionnaire for this survey will not be available for the respondents.
