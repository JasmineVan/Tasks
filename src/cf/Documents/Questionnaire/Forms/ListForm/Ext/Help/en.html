Use this window to monitor the survey progress.

An employee responsible for the survey can track and monitor it in real time. Questionnaires filled in by respondents get onto the list.

To open the list, click Questionnaires in the Survey section.

The list includes the following columns:

  • Date is the date and time when the respondent finished editing the questionnaire.
  • Number is the questionnaire number, which is assigned automatically.
  • Survey is information on the Survey purpose document.
  • Respondent is the description of External user, who is a respondent.

View questionnaire

  • You can open a questionnaire, for example, to check its completeness. To do so, double-click the questionnaire.
