Use this window for responses to questions when conducting a survey.

To open, double-click it in the questionnaire list, which becomes available when the respondent starts the application.

Questionnaire sections

  • On the first page of the questionnaire, you can see a text from the introductory section.
  • To move around the questionnaire sections, click Next and Back located in the upper-right corner of the window (recommended).
  • You can also use the navigation list (tree) of sections, the visibility of which is set up by clicking Show sections/Hide sections, to move around the sections.
    • It is recommended that you open the section tree to view the questionnaire structure or select a section if necessary. To show the questionnaire items correctly, it is recommended that you hide the section tree.
    • You can double-click to select one of the questionnaire sections in the tree. Once the questionnaire section is selected, the section filling window is generated.
  • At the end of the questionnaire, there is the final section.

Ways to respond to questions

  • Depending on the question type, an answer can be written manually, selected from the list, or selected using the radio button (tumbler).
  • Dates can be either written manually or selected from the calendar by clicking .
  • Answers to some questions must be entered into the table. In some tables, you need to Add rows by clicking the appropriate button.

Saving the questionnaire

  • If a questionnaire can be saved, the Write button appears. Use this button to save a partially filled questionnaire to return to its filling later.
  • If a questionnaire was filled in before but was not completed, the date of its latest edit is specified in the list of available questionnaires.
  • Click Close to close the questionnaire without saving.

Finish editing the questionnaire

  • To finish editing the questionnaire, click Complete and close.
  • A message indicating that further questionnaire filling will be unavailable appears.
  • To confirm, click Yes. Click No to be able to come back to questionnaire filling again. Once confirmed, the questionnaire disappears from the list of available questionnaires, but you can view it by clicking Questionnaire archive (it depends on the questionnaire settings).

Required questions

  • A questionnaire author can introduce required questions. Fields for responses to such questions are highlighted in red.
  • If you do not give answers to required questions, an automatic check is carried out when you try to complete questionnaire editing and a message about required questions that are unanswered appears.
  • The respondent will be able to finish questionnaire editing once they answer all required questions.
