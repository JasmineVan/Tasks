Entering info on a group of notes.

You can group the All notes list. To do this, create a required number of groups and drag notes to them. Also, when you enter a note, you can select the group to which it belongs.

Entering a group of notes

You can group notes by the subjects they belong to, or by topics.

  • Enter the group description in the Subject field.
  • You can subordinate a new group to one of the existing groups. To do this, click Show all in the Notes group and select a group from the list.
  • The application populates the Author field with the current user. You cannot change this field.
