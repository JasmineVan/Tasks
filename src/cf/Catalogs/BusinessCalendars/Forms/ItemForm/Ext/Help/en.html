View or generate a business calendar.

Business calendar is a kind of classifier, permanent information that does not change during the year.

The Business calendar of the Russian Federation (which is nationwide) is included in the delivered application data and updated as the calendar is approved and holidays are substituted in the Russian Federation.

You can enter more than one business calendar, if you need to. There can be several business calendars as holidays and preholiday days vary in different regions.

It is recommended to enter the business calendar at the beginning of the year on the basis of the national calendar for the whole year at once. By doing this you will avoid errors in calculations throughout the year.

Business calendar itself is not used in accounting. Work schedules of departments, as well as work schedules of employees are generated on the basis of the business calendar.

New business calendar input

  • Fill in the business calendar title:
    • Description.
    • Code (not more than 3 characters);
    • Year (is currrent by default).

Regional calendar

  • If you need to enter a new calendar that depends on the federal one, select the Is regional calendar check box, and select the federal calendar from the dropdown list.

Change calendar days

  • The new business calendar has days off.
  • Click Change day and arrange calendar days.
    • Day kinds in a business calendar are color-marked as follows:
      • black - workdays,
      • dark red - Saturdays,
      • red - Sundays,
      • dark blue - preholiday days,
      • purple - holidays.

Move a holiday to another day

This feature makes sense if you need the calendar data, but officially it has not yet been approved and the application does not have it, because the government has not approved all substitute holidays. You can fill the substitute holidays earlier, and then, when the data of the national calendar is approved and included in the application delivery, the substitute holidays will be automatically adjusted.

  • Select a holiday that you want to move in the business calendar.
  • Click Move day
  • Select the date of a substitute holiday in the calendar, click OK.
  • Information on moved days is displayed in the Move days, located in the right part of the business calendar.

Default filling

  • You can use the Fill in by default command to fill in the calendar:
    • If the Is a regional calendar check box is selected, the calendar is filled in based on business calendar included in the application.
      • For the current year, the automatic generation of the business calendar is made on the basis of already approved information on workdays, holidays and moved holidays, for future years calendars are generated according to the Labor Code of the Russian Federation (for the Russian business calendar).
    • If the Is a regional calendar check box is not selected, the calendar is filled in based on the Labor Code of the Russian Federation.

Print business calendar

  • Click Print to display summary data of the business calendar for the specified year.
    • You can display information on the annual balance of working time, as well as balance by month, quarter, and halfyear.
    • The application displays statistics on the number of calendar days, workdays, weekends and holidays, as well as working hours in hours with a 40-hour, 36-hour and 24-hour working week.
