It is designed to generate and store business calendars.

To open it, click the corresponding command in the Catalogs section.

Business calendar is a kind of classifier, permanent information that does not change during the year. There can be several business calendars as holidays and preholiday days vary in different regions.

The Business calendar of the Russian Federation (which is nationwide) is included in the delivered application data and updated as the calendar is approved and holidays are substituted in the Russian Federation. You can enter more than one business calendar, if you need to. It is recommended to enter the business calendar at the beginning of the year on the basis of the national calendar for the whole year at once. By doing this you will avoid errors in calculations throughout the year.

Business calendars themselves are not used in accounting. Work schedules of departments, as well as work schedules of employees are generated on the basis of the business calendar.

Business calendar input

  • Click Create and select the way of creating a business calendar:
    • By classifier. It is recommended to add calendar from the classifier The application is delivered with the business calendar classifier.
    • New (enter manually (if the needed calendar is missing from the classifier)).
  • Fill in the required fields.
