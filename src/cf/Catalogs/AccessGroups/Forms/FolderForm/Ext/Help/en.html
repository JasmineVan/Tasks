Description of access group folder properties.

To facilitate management of multiple access groups with similar rights restrictions and avoid confusion, you can create folders to simplify the search for the required access group and to understand the access right restrictions in general.

Access group folders can be organized by a variety of criteria.

Creating access group folders

  • Enter Folder description;
  • You can make a folder subordinate to a Parent group (folder). Select a folder from the list and click Select. Or double-click the parent folder.
