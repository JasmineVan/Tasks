This list is used to store the accounting check rules.

To open the list, click the Accounting check rules hyperlink in the Administration – Service and maintenance – Data adjustment section.

The list includes the following columns:

  • Check usage (the checks in use have selected check boxes)
  • Description
  • Check schedule
  • Issue severity

Check methods

  • Manual. Click Execute to run the manual check.
  • On general schedule. To modify the schedule, click the hyperlink at the bottom of the window. If the schedule is filled, clocking the hyperlink opens the schedule.
  • On individual schedule. Double-click to open the check, and modify the required fields.

Reviewing the discovered issues

  • The check results are displayed in the Accounting control report.
  • To view the report:
    • Click Reports – Accounting check results
    • Open the problem with the issues
    • Open the To-do list on the home page.
